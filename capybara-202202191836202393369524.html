Chris Hewitt

is a percussionist, composer and developer located in Denver, CO. Chris's playing and compositions are informed by the jazz tradition, contemporary Western art music, pop, rock,and the music of Indonesia. Chris is a graduate of the prestigious Colorado State University percussion studio, and is currently studying back end engineering at the Turing School of Software and Design.
